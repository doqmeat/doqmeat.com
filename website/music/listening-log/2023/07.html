    KARMA(a)DARMA - abu-se-ken

    type: album
    genres: art rock?
    date released: 18 july 2023

    this was pretty damn cool. while its not exactly my cup of tea, i did enjoy the cohesiveness of the songs while also exploring different sounds. and i found the intermission songs, like ignite I and ignite II, pretty cool since i havent really heard something like from this producer.

    the previously released vocaloid songs that are in this album like judas and want to be cremated fit very nicely into the musical theme (ik this has a specific name i think but rn i cant remember sorry) of the album.
    overall i had a really nice time with this album even tho i mostly just vibed with the previously released vocaloid songs but that didnt prevent me from enjoying it as a whole. i might up the rating the more i listen to it.

    oh actually the last song bush was really cool i really liked that song.
    also super cool to see they opened up a bandcamp! cant wait to see what else they release in the future! abuseken is one of those voca producers that have a very different and distinct style and i really like that about their music

    highlights: judas, christ, bush, want to be cremated
