13.apr.26 i don't remember where i first saw this game at. but just the look of this game combined with the amazing composer that is disasterpeace sold me on it. i played the demo recently and i loved it so so so much. i can't wait for october!!! the protag looks very interesting and the horror bits are so neat. the action part is a little bit rough on me but i think that's because i was playing it with a mouse and keyboard. i will most likely get it on my switch once it comes out. i honestly felt so entranced and immersed while playing it. it made me feel like when i played hyper light drifter for the first time. but maybe that's the pixel art and disasterpeace.

have i ever mentioned that i fucking love pixel art?
