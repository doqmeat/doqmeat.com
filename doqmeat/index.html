hello! my name is des, and this right here is my very own corner of the internet dedicated to the things that i love. i mostly use it as a digital journal, of both my personal life and interests.

doqmeat.com is 100% a passion project about self expression. this site is for myself first, though i don't mind sharing it with the world wide web! which is why i am committed to making it as responsive and accessible to the best of my abilities.

quick notes about site:

  • uses javascript and iframes. for fun stuff and site functionality.
  • there is no explicit content on my site but keep in mind it is made by an adult with an adult audience in mind.
  • i update my site a lot. hit that CTRL + F5 if something looks wonky. if that doesn't fix it feel free to e-mail me.
  • yes, my domain is a reference to fallout's dogmeat! despite being a huge cat person, i love that dog.
