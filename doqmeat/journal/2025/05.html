hello everypony! it's been a while since i've been here! i was super busy with uni which is why i didn't update so much throughout these past 2 months.

i feel like this semester i got to open up a bit more to people. i met a lot of new people and got closer to others. which is very nice as someone who struggles to make friends. i also got to join my uni's organization for and ran by computer science students which has made me talk to new people. i really hope i can continue doing that. having an easier time meeting new people and such.

this was a pretty fun semester but man did it felt so busy! which is probably why i feel like this one passed by the fastest. it was probably calculus. any free time i would have i would dedicate it to that class. which was the class that brought me the most pain this semester. i sort of like math not gonna lie. but oh my goodness. it took A LOT from me for things to finally click, which they did like 4 days before the final YAY throughout the semester i had 3 partial exams. and in all 3 of them i got C's but i feel pretty confident on the final so i am hoping for a B and worst case scenario a high C! i still have to take calculus 2 for my degree so i am not quite done with calculus yet. i do want a break so i will be taking discrete math next semester.

out of all the classes i was taking, my highlight was my intro to programming class. of course. i'm gonna sound so nerdy but i always looked forward to this class. i learned so many things (a little bit more in depth) that i was already a bit familiarized with and how they worked and why they worked. i also really like the professor as well. i feel like i talked too much in that class because i was mainly the one asking questions and stuff. but oh well, at least i was having fun with the class! honestly it was lowkey a bit of a breeze but i still got really engaged with the class nonetheless.
