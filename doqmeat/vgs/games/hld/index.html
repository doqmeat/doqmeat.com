hyper light drifter

release date: mar 31, 2016
developer: heart machine
genre/type: action, adventure

one of my fav games ever. i wish i could play this game for the first time. it has such an amazing atmosphere thanks to the beautiful pixel art and soundtrack. it can be so haunting at times and yet so peaceful. and the combat is just a blast as well. i remember my first time playing i was getting my ass kicked left and right but now i slay (literally) every year that i replay it. i truly love this game a lot and i consider it to be my fav video game of all time hyper light drifter i love you!!!!!!
