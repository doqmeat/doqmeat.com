sorry for doing this mid 2025, i simply just didn't have a lot of motivation to do this at the end of 2024 (had the flu and then the massive outage). so i kinda forgot to do this... but also another reason is bc i simply didn't listen to that many new releases in 2024 because of life and it's still something that i struggle with bc i love listening to new music but i'm more busy as of late! i thought that making a 2024 recap would feel a bit underwhelming compared to other years so i ignored it. but then i thought about it and i still had a few releases to highlight from that year so here we are.

favorite songs

worm in the apple - youra

such an addictive song... it does kinda make me feel like a worm

天使がいたよ - daoko

a very sweet but energetic song. this just has all the sounds that i love tbh.

loop - yves

an amazing debut from yves! to me this is literally the best song in her discography to date. the feature on this song did take a while to marinate for me but i eventually saw the charm and vision. it does work.

24 - tripleS

LOVE LOVE LOVE AT FIRST LISTEN! the instrumental alongside the song is just one of the best. when i'm in a good mood i always dance to this song.

aqua - JO1

been there for me in A LOT of train rides.

出発の朝 - inuha

a very sweet and touching song. i need to look up the lyrics though idk what miku is saying but i trust her.

magnetic - illit

i got HOOKED on this song because of that video that delayed the instrumental by 7 seconds but then i eventually downloaded the EP and it honestly just kinda clicked. the whole EP probably last like 6 mins but it's still really nice and cohesive.

girls' night - loossemble

just pure happiness man i miss them.

schizodipshit - machine girl

fun and ugly

taste - the deep / h4rdy

super cutieful

vampire in the corner - magdalena bay

I WANNA LEARN HOW TO LOVE!!! i should put this entire project here because of my god. honestly one of the best listening experiences i've had with an album. besides this song i really liked fear, sex as well.

the hitchhiker's guide to the galaxy - ARTMS

THIS IS ALL I WANT!!! i just want fun eletropop kpop songs oh my lorddd.

i'll see you there tomorrow - TXT

possibly the only song from a hybe conglomerate boy group that i enjoyed that year

satellite - youha

satisfied - kim woo-seok
