    unlock my word - fromis_9

    type: album
    genres: kpop, dancepop, house
    date released: 5 june 2023

    this was great. what a nice kpop album. very consistent and cohesive throughout its run time too. i was expecting to just like 1 song or 2 from this but there were a lot of tracks that are so enjoyable! though i really think attitude is such an insane opening track. i loved that song so much. the production is just excelent. actually, this 36 mins (i think) album took me like an hour to listen to completely because i just kept listening to attitude again and again.

    the house / uk garage influences some that the bsides have just MUAHHH so good. i think thats why i found this album so enjoyable. the title track is a pretty nice nu-disco song, but its not something you havent heard. in the past year in kpop, given how it has been a literal trend. so thats why the title track wasnt anything wow for me. but some of the bsides??? wow!!!! which is why songs like don't care and prom night are very refreshing and right up my alley.
    and besides those i also really liked my night routine because of how chill it is. its something i would put in my sleeping playlist. love songd like that.

    to conclude this, i really enjoyed this. i think i might up the rating to 4 stars if i listen to it but for now this will do

    highlights: attitude, dont care, my night routine, prom night

    rating:

    listened on 8.jun.23
