my beautiful madoka dics01.jun.25

during my last madoka magica rewatch on december 2023 i was thinking about how ass it is that i have have to watch this through some shady ass anime site where the eng subtitles barely work so i had to watch the eng dub instead. like damn that sucks! and i'm not paying for a crunchyroll subscription either! so i decided to eventually start looking for physical versions of the anime and rebellion once i had a bit of money to spend.

while looking though ebay i noticed that some region A blu-rays of madoka were AWFULLY a bit expensive. especially the box sets. i initially thought that physical versions of PMMM were simply out of stock though their official stores. and this is not the case btw, you can get some sets over at the crunchyroll store... at Normal prices.

one day i stumbled upon a listing of a madoka blu-ray labeled PMMM Disc I & II. the listing mention it was from a box set but my foolish ass didn't bother to look more into it. you see, i was thinking that disc I & II had PMMM beginnings and eternal respectively. or at least 6 episodes on each disc. but then the blu-ray arrived and um. it was not what i was thinking!

each disc has 4 episodes. meaning that i was missing the last final 4 episodes of the anime. honestly for the price of $40 i decided to keep it bc i don't think the anime (not the movie versions of the anime) is getting any more blu-rays anytime soon. there are minor differences between the movies and the anime, bit at least i have the iconic scene of hitomi going girls can't love girls!!! (happy pride month btw ) with this purchase since that scene is missing in the movie version of the anime. i did some more digging and i found the disc i was missing. unfortunately the only listing of it (separately) i'm seeing on ebay is at $108 and there is no way i'm touching that at that price. i'm gonna keep being in the look out for it nonetheless and if i get another madoka disc, i'll write another log about it.

i eventually got the limited edition rebellion blu-ray for a pretty nice price and MAN is that how you do packaging. this has to be one of the most gorgeous and detailed packages that i've ever seen. i love the whole theater / play motif that is used in the series as well. whoever designed this did such a great job. it includes 3 dics: the movie, an extra disc with bonus features, and the OST. the OST was a very pleasant surprise to have here. i listened to it on my portable CD player while hanging clothes and man... what a beautiful score. it's so delicate. my favorite song from there is possibly this is my despair.

it also included a material book with an interview from members of the crew, inu curry artworks, storyboards, other art and posters! i loved seeing these very much, specially the inu curry artworks. to my luck everything has been translated to eng by the puella magi wiki. thank god i dont have to photo translate this because it is lowkey incomprehensible when i tried to do it.

on my 6th watching of rebellion i am still noticing new things about the film. watching this on my tv via my PS5 who works part time as a blu-ray player feels SO awesome. i like owning blu-rays of my favorite movies (should do a list of the ones i own someday on my site!) so these dics were a much necessary addition to my collection since i'm always on shady places to be able to watch them. i love physical media.
