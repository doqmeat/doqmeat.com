cloud gardens

release date: september 9, 2020
developer: noio
genre/type: puzzle

logs

11.apr.24 got it early access one day bc i loved the concept around it. it's basically a puzzle game about growing plants on a abandoned... place or something like that. the real charm of this game is honestly in it's sound design and soundtrack and how well it goes with the gameplay. i used to play this so much when i would be having it rough, and it never failed to calm me down a bit. i also love the pixely art style is soooo cute. i love this game a lot.
