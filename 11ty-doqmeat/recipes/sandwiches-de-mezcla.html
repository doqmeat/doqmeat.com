sandwiches de mezcla: a popular party plate in puerto rican culture. i fucking love these sandwiches to death i could probably eat a whole loaf of bread of these in one sitting.

ingredients

  • cheese dip, i used cheez whiz
  • bell pepper, we use the ones that come in cans
  • luncheon meat, pork
  • mayonnaise
  • bread slices

tools:

  • a blender

procedure

  1. put everything in the blender. (EXCEPT THE BREAD SLICES)
  2. orange goop
  3. spread orange goop in a sandwiches
  4. cut corners
  5. cut a cross in the sandwich so its cuts into 4 pieces
  6. delicious orange goop sandwiches! it should look like this
