    webmaster.txt

    the name is des i'm a 25 year old from puerto rico. fluent in spanish and english so feel free to talk me to in either! i go by he / she * pronouns though at the end of the day you can call me whatever, i don't mind. i'm a 3rd year computer science student interested in web development and web design i love life.

    （*＾-＾*）this is also me ->

    interest that don't involve having fun coding and design include listening to music, walking, journaling, petting my cat, and kissing my girlfriend.

    this site started as something to mess around with when bored around april 2023 but as time passed i fell more and more in love with the idea of having a space where i can customize every corner of it and call it my home. i am mostly here to share my thoughts, archive things important to me, and have fun! this site is mainly just for me and i love maintaining it when i have some free time. it's my own journal i can show to anyone. this hobby also led me to pursue my degree in computer science which i'm also very greatful for!

    i won't reach out fast but do feel free to e-mail me at meow@doqmeat.com about whatever, really! whether it be to tell me about what you thought about my site, to send me pictures of your cats, recommend me an album you think i might like, or even ask any coding / website related questions you might have. i might take a while to answer back but anything goes, thank you very much for reading!
