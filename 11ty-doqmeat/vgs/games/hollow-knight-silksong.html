18.jul.26 one of the best single player experiences i've ever had. holy fucking shit. this is also another game that i love but also makes me suffer with it's difficulty. but you know what, that is is where most of the fun comes from. i thank god for the different crests in the game that let you change the moveset because while hornet's moveset is really cool and the diagonal jump is a bit handy at times... i DO NOT have the patience to use it when it comes to the platforming jumping sections of the game. SORRY. reaper crest 4ever.

i recently beat the last judge (took me 3 days but i did it yay) and while underworks was kind of crazy, i almost cried hearing the score when entering choral chambers like wow wow wow wow...

i will probably go back to bloodborne once i am done with silksong. i simply just can't handle two suffering games at the same time.
